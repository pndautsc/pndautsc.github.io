About PNDA





WHO WE ARE



Hey there! We’re happy you made it to our website!


A little bit about us… The PNDA is a student-run group focused on enriching the academic, professional, and personal development of the UTSC student body. Our purpose is to serve as a common and inclusive hub for all students, faculty, and staff of the psychology, neuroscience, and mental health studies programs by providing resources and platforms for discussion, networking, and opportunities related to those fields and beyond.

Upcoming Events

TUESDAY, APRIL 6TH


Tuesday Talks

Tuesday Talks allow students to interact with professors in a casual setting. We invite one guest professor every week and open the floor to students to ask their questions, letting the discussions flow. These can touch on anything from research to hobbies to life advice. Sessions are facilitated by members of the PNDA executive team.

WEDNESDAY, APRIL 28TH


Games Night

Games Nights are an invitation for all UTSC students to relax and socialize while engaging in friendly competition. Prizes are usually up for grabs! Sessions are held regularly (i.e., weekly or biweekly) and typically feature a collaboration with other campus groups.

FRIDAY, MARCH 31ST


Academic Research Panel

The Academic Research Panel promotes neuroscience, psychology, and mental health research at UTSC by providing students and faculty with a platform to present and discuss their projects. This is a two-day event that takes place yearly. Sessions are facilitated by members of the PNDA executive team.
